About YIGA

Our Story

Youth in Global Affairs (YIGA) is a youth-led civil society organization that champions meaningful youth engagement with the world's most critical global challenges.

We empower young people to think, act, and lead through evidence-based research, capacity-building trainings, dynamic workshops, intellectual dialogues, and conferences. By equipping the youth with the skills, knowledge, and platforms they need to contribute to policy and public discourse, YIGA fosters a generation of informed, innovative, and responsible global actors.

We are guided by the conviction that youth—who have long stood at the forefront of social and political transformation—must be intentionally equipped to sustain and amplify that impact.

Our Core Objectives

1 Research Excellence

Encourage and support the production of high-quality, youth-driven research on critical global challenges.

2 Capacity Building

Organize trainings, workshops, and conferences to equip young people with knowledge and skills relevant to our focus areas.

3 Policy Engagement

Advance meaningful youth involvement in foreign policy processes and international relations.

4 Inclusive Spaces

Create inclusive spaces for thoughtful discourse, idea-sharing, and collaboration among youth and global affairs stakeholders.

Our Focus Areas

Foreign Policy

We engage youth in understanding and shaping international relations, diplomatic processes, and global governance mechanisms. Our programs help young people develop the skills needed to navigate complex international systems.

Good Governance

We promote transparency, accountability, and effective institutions at all levels. Our initiatives focus on building youth capacity to engage with governance processes and advocate for reforms.

Climate Change

We address environmental challenges through youth-led research, advocacy, and action. Our programs empower young people to contribute to climate policy and sustainable development.

Peace and Security

We foster dialogue, conflict resolution, and peacebuilding among youth. Our initiatives help young people develop skills to prevent conflicts and build more peaceful societies.

Culture and Heritage

We work to preserve and promote cultural diversity, heritage, and intercultural dialogue. Our programs help young people appreciate and leverage cultural resources for sustainable development.
